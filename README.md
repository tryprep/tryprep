## Hi there ! 👋  

I'm Maria Carolina Elias 🧬

I am a **Principal Investigator and Associate Professor** based at **Instituto Butantan**, Brazil. My research is dedicated to uncovering the molecular mechanisms governing **DNA replication**, **cell cycle control**, and **genomic stability**, primarily in Trypanosomatids (*Trypanosoma cruzi* and *Trypanosoma brucei*).

---

### 🔬 Research Focus

My work bridges the gap between molecular biology and bioinformatics to understand how parasites maintain and diversify their genomes:

- **DNA Replication in Trypanosomatids:** Identification and characterization of replication origins, assembly of the pre-replication complex, and the role of replication stress in genetic variability.
- **Genome Maintenance:** Investigating the DNA damage response (DDR) and the proteins involved in homologous recombination and chromatin organization (e.g., Rad51, Topoisomerase 3α).

---

### 🚀 Current Projects

*   **[FAPESP Thematic Project]** Investigating the contribution of DNA replication to the genetic variability of *Trypanosoma cruzi*.
*   **[CeVIVAS]** Coordination of genomic surveillance for respiratory and emerging viruses in the State of São Paulo.
*   **[Bioinformatics Pipelines]** Development of automated tools for viral identification and variant monitoring (e.g., VIPER pipeline).
